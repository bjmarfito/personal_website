Earth Observatory of Singapore — NTU

Bryan Marfito

Earthquake Scientist & Geophysicist

I use geodetic methods, including Interferometric Synthetic Aperture Radar (InSAR), to investigate the characteristics of faults responsible for destructive earthquakes in Asia. I also analyze post-earthquake deformation observed by InSAR and GNSS to better understand fault mechanics. My goal is to contribute to advances in tectonics and earthquake science that can help improve societal resilience to damaging earthquakes.
